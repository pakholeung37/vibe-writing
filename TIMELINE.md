# Timeline

Chronological events for "The Gray Inheritance"

---

## Backstory Timeline

### 30 Years Ago
- [[Victoria Gray]] marries [[Richard Gray]]
- The Grays are old money, highly respected
- Victoria comes from modest background

### 25 Years Ago
- [[Marcus Wells]] born to Victoria's sister
- Victoria and Richard have no children of their own

### 15 Years Ago
- Richard Gray dies under mysterious circumstances
- Ruled natural causes (heart failure)
- Victoria inherits the entire Gray estate
- Rumors circulate but nothing proven

### 10 Years Ago
- Marcus's parents die in car accident
- Victoria becomes his guardian briefly
- Relationship sours quickly
- Marcus leaves, becomes estranged

### 5 Years Ago
- [[Elena Ross]] hired as Victoria's personal assistant
- Victoria begins philanthropic work
- Plans for [[The Gray Foundation]] first discussed

### 1 Year Ago
- Victoria's health begins declining
- [[Dr. Howard Price]] becomes her regular physician
- Victoria starts reviewing her will

### 6 Months Ago
- Marcus contacts Victoria asking for money
- Victoria refuses—their last communication
- Marcus's gambling debts worsen

### 1 Month Ago
- Victoria secretly contacts lawyer about will change
- Plans to leave everything to [[The Gray Foundation]]
- Tells no one except her lawyer

## Story Timeline

### Day 0 (Night Before Discovery)

**Evening**
- Victoria has dinner alone
- [[Elena Ross]] retires to her quarters at 9 PM
- [[Marcus Wells]] arrives unexpectedly around 10 PM
- Argument heard by [[Mrs. Patterson]] (housekeeper)
- Marcus leaves (or does he?)

**Night**
- Victoria works late in her study
- The murder occurs between 11 PM and 1 AM
- Digitalis administered (method TBD)

### Day 1 (Discovery)

**6:00 AM**
- [[Mrs. Patterson]] arrives to begin work
- Notes nothing unusual

**7:30 AM**
- [[Elena Ross]] brings Victoria's morning tea
- Finds Victoria dead at her desk
- Calls emergency services

**8:15 AM**
- Police arrive, secure the scene
- Initial assessment: natural causes

**9:00 AM**
- [[Detective Chen]] catches the case
- Something feels wrong—requests investigation
- [Chapter 1: The Call]

**11:00 AM**
- Chen arrives at [[Gray Manor]]
- Examines the scene, interviews Elena
- [Chapter 2: The Scene begins]

**2:00 PM**
- [[Marcus Wells]] arrives at the manor
- Demands to know what happened
- Chen notes his nervousness

### Day 2 (Initial Investigation)

- Interviews with household staff
- Background research on the Grays
- Chen learns about [[The Gray Inheritance]]
- [Chapter 3: The Family]

### Day 3

- Deep interview with [[Elena Ross]]
- Her alibi, her history, her grief
- [Chapter 4: The Assistant]

### Day 4

- Focus on [[Marcus Wells]]
- Discovering his debts, his desperation
- His shaky alibi for the night
- [Chapter 5: The Nephew]

### Day 5

- Forensics report returns
- Digitalis poisoning confirmed
- Case officially becomes homicide
- [Chapter 6: The Evidence]

### Day 6

- Research into Gray family history
- Richard Gray's death reexamined
- Victoria's fears uncovered
- [Chapter 7: The Past]

### Day 7

- Discovery of hidden documents
- The secret will change revealed
- [[The Gray Foundation]] connection
- [Chapter 8: The Secret]

### Day 8 (Resolution)

- Confrontation at [[Gray Manor]]
- All parties gathered
- [Chapter 9: The Confrontation]

- Killer revealed and arrested
- [Chapter 10: The Truth]
