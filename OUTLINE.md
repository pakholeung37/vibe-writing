# The Gray Inheritance - Story Outline

## Premise

Detective Chen is called to [[Gray Manor]] after wealthy [[Victoria Gray]] is found dead in her study. The investigation reveals a web of secrets, debts, and betrayals centered on the Gray family fortune. The prime suspects are Victoria's estranged nephew [[Marcus Wells]] and her devoted personal assistant [[Elena Ross]].

## Themes

- Inherited guilt and family legacy
- The corrosive nature of wealth
- Loyalty vs. self-preservation
- Truth hidden in plain sight
